Experience

Nick Bromer has designed and patented products in fields as varied as augmented reality interfaces and imaging, 3D printing, holography, medical devises, carbon nanotubes, LED-based diffuse lighting and communication systems, reinforced concrete, loudspeakers, skate brakes, ceramic cutting edges, architecture, clothing, and laser and mirror visual projections. He specializes in designing elegant and effective products, with an emphasis on mechanical/technical efficiency. His work has been featured in the New York Times as well as American Inventions by Stephen van Dulken.



His long tenure as a skilled patent attorney has attuned him to the intellectual property considerations that must be recognized when creating and marketing a new product. Working under contract for Intellectual Ventures, an intellectual property company with a large portfolio of patents, he submitted 78 creative and robust product solutions in response to specific requests. Overall, he has 15 issued patents. In addition to his extensive experience, he has a wide network of highly accomplished professionals in the fields of intellectual property, materials science, engineering, and design.
