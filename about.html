About Nick

Nick Bromer is a consultant who specializes in bringing the ideas and ambitions of his clients to fruition. His wealth of technical know-how and his creative faculty allow him to drive toward elegant and effective solutions. Working side by side with your product team, Nick will help you to go from being a competitor in an overcrowded market to being a pioneer in a landscape of untapped market potential. He emphasizes the need to develop patentable technology that is truly new and innovative.



Working under contract, he will leverage his extensive experience with inventing custom products, as well as his network of accomplished professionals in the fields of intellectual property, materials science, and aesthetic design, to create custom products that are specifically designed to suit your needs.
